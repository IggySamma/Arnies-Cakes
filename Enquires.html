Policy:

We kindly request that all orders be placed at least two weeks in advance.
If an order is placed with short notice, a short-notice fee may apply.
We offer delivery services to most surrounding areas, subject to an additional fee.

Please specify the desired collection date when submitting your enquire.
To secure your selected date, a deposit of 50% is required upon booking.
Should you need to cancel your order, we kindly ask that you provide us with sufficient notice, failure to comply will result in a non-refundable deposit.

We will try to respond as soon as possible.
