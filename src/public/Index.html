About Me

Hi! I'm Arnie.

Growing up I always dreamt of how amazing it would be to go to work every day to a place that smelled of fresh cakes and colorfully decorated treats. I put this dream on the backburner, until the beginning of 2020.

I found so much comfort in baking cakes in my home kitchen. My friends and family started wondering how they could order and taste what I was baking, and that's how Arnie's Cakes was born!

Since then, I have spent the years learning and baking, creating fun treats for so many special occasions, and doing what I love.
